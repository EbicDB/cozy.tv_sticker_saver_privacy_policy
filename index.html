Cozy.tv Sticker Saver Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2023

Cozy.tv Sticker Saver is designed with privacy in mind, it does not access any authentication data or personally identifiable information. All extension data is stored locally on your computer and is never transmitted over the network. The Cozy.tv Sticker Saver extension provides the ability to save your favorite stickers for use across all Cozy.tv channels. To facilitate this, the extension captures your clicks and modifies Cozy.tv HTML.

Disclaimer: Cozy.tv Sticker Saver is not affiliated with Cozy.tv
