Cozy.tv Sticker Saver Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2023

The Cozy.tv Sticker Saver extension provides the ability to save your favorite stickers for use across all Cozy.tv channels. To facilitate this, the extension captures your clicks and modifies Cozy.tv HTML. All Cozy.tv Sticker Saver data is stored locally on your computer and is never transmitted over the network. Cozy.tv Sticker Saver is not affiliated with Cozy.tv
